About

An unofficial, fan-made training ground for FiveM-style vault hacks. Every minigame is rebuilt from scratch to mirror the real mechanics, visuals and difficulty — so you can practice the patterns until they're muscle memory.

Is this the real game?

No — it's a practice replica. Not affiliated with or endorsed by any game.

How do I play?

Pick a hack to drill it solo, or hit Run Full Breach to chain all four against the clock.

What happens if I fail the breach?

Fail the terminal and it restarts; fail anything after and you're sent back to the start.

Keyboard or mouse?

Both — mouse for routing & pathing, keyboard for the decoder, and both for the terminal.
